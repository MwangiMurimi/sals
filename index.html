About Us

Salvador "Sal" Soprano and his sons Antonio and Vito founded Sal's in 1930 in New York. Their signature pizzas, coupled with the famous Sal's Secret Sauce propelled them to legendary status.

On December 12, 2000, the first Sal's Pizza Africa was opened right here in Nairobi, on Loita Street. Our time in Kenya has been nothing short of amazing.

With the Build Your Own Pizza campaign here, our only one worldwide, we are glad to contribute to a country that has contributed significantly to our transformation from an Italian immigrant's dream to a wordlwide brand.
